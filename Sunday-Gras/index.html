Short Description Text

SUNDAY GRAS is a New Orleans’ based concert that takes place the Sunday before Fat Tuesday. The show was created in 2016 to be a late-night post parade event geared towards 18-24 year olds, highlighting the Big Easy’s non-stop rager march towards Mardi Gras. SUNDAY GRAS has now evolved into a life-style brand that combines the global youth movement with the music and multi-cultural spirit of Mardi Gras in New Orleans, LA.
